About CoreAxis

Established on October 25, 2025, CoreAxis Advisory Partners (CAP) is a multidisciplinary professional advisory and consultancy firm based in Dhaka, Bangladesh.

Guided by our founding partners, Ms. Shayema Akter (Chartered Accountant) and Mr. Mohammed Azizul Haq, we provide independent and ethical advisory services. Our goal is to empower organizations with actionable insights, robust financial frameworks, and unparalleled compliance strategies locally and internationally.
